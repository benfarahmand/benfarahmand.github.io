Own Your Factory's Data

Real-time counts. Simple install. No subscriptions.

Designed for growing manufacturers who want modern production visibility without expensive enterprise platforms.

The Problem

Many manufacturers still walk the floor to count boxes and estimate if they’re on track. By the time issues are discovered, they’ve already cost time and money.

The Solution

A compact, factory‑ready device and dashboard that:

  • Counts production in real time (from your existing sensors or our recommended ones)
  • Shows the right product to run (photo + bill of materials) so operators stay aligned
  • Works entirely on your network (no data leaves your facility)

Three Core Pillars

  1. No Subscriptions:
    One‑time purchase. Own the hardware and software. Optional support only if you want it.
  2. Simple Setup:
    Raspberry Pi‑based unit with a 10″ touchscreen. Mount it, connect power and a sensor cable, open the dashboard. No IT department needed.
  3. Real‑Time Counts:
    Live production counters by line or SKU, visible on the shop floor and accessible via a simple dashboard.

Outcomes You Can Expect

  • Stop guessing: know current totals vs. targets at a glance
  • Catch issues earlier (mis‑counts, wrong SKU)
  • Eliminate manual box counting and end‑of‑shift surprises
  • Create traceable digital records (CSV exports + simple API)

What's Included

  • Hardware:
    Industrial mini‑PC (Raspberry Pi 5) with 10″ touch display, factory enclosure, power supply, sensor connector(s)
  • Software:
    Local web dashboard for line counts and product setup (photos + BOM)
  • Connectivity:
    Ethernet or Wi‑Fi
  • Data Access:
    CSV export and lightweight REST endpoints for integration
  • Sensor Compatibility:
    Default support for Keyence PZ‑G62CB and PR‑G41CBL (NPN, 12 V logic) via included adapter. Other photoelectric / proximity sensors supported.

Why It’s Better

  • No subscription required. You own the hardware, forever.
  • Custom-fit. Each unit is tailored to your exact sensor and signal specs. Out of the box, the system supports Keyence PZ-G62CB and PR-G41CBL sensors via a tested M8 adapter connected to the Raspberry Pi's GPIO.
  • Instant visibility. View widget-by-widget analytics on a built-in screen.
  • REST API or Local Dashboard. Connect to your internal systems via REST or log in directly over LAN.
  • Simpler than the market leader. No bloated software, no IT overhead.

Technical Specs

  • Computing Platform: Raspberry Pi 5
  • Display: 10” touchscreen
  • Keyence Sensor Adapter: 12V logic-compatible input via M8 connector + voltage divider or optoisolator.
  • Data Access: REST endpoint (JSON format) for pre-existing system integrations or a local dashboard hosted on the Raspberry Pi over LAN depending on your needs
  • Connectivity: Wi-Fi and Ethernet
  • Storage: microSD (default) with SSD upgrade available

Compatibility & Integration

  • Default Sensor Support: Keyence PZ-G62CB, PR-G41CBL (NPN, 12V logic)
  • Signal Types: NPN or 12V digital logic with included adapter
  • Connector: M8 4-pin with included custom wiring adapter

Optional Add-ons:

  • White-labeled branding (your logo on the enclosure and on the dashboard)
  • Additional sensor support based on your needs
  • Multi-color beacon light for quick visibility of line status from a distance
  • Power-over-Ethernet integration
  • Custom dashboards and remote access setup
