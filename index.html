The Problem

For example, a line may be running below target pace for hours without anyone noticing until the shift ends.

FAQ

Are sensors included?

Sensors are not included, but many customers already have compatible sensors on hand. If not, we can recommend cost-effective options or work with your existing supplier.

What sensors has it been tested with?

Tested with Keyence PZ-G62CB and PR-G41CBL sensors.

What if my line uses a different sensor?

We can review your sensor setup with you and work out the right way to support it.

Does it require cloud access or a subscription?

No. It runs locally on your network and does not require a subscription.

How is it powered and connected?

Ethernet is the recommended default because PoE can simplify power and networking with one cable. Wi-Fi is also available if needed.

Can it be repaired or serviced?

Yes. It is built from serviceable off-the-shelf components rather than proprietary locked-down hardware, making replacement and repair straightforward.
